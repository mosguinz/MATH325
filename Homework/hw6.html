2. Determine if the following statements are true or false. Give explanation.

(a) Suppose that there are 6 vectors in R4\R^4R4, it must be linearly decpendent.

True. Only four linearly independent vectors are needed to span R4\R^4R4.

(b) Suppose that there are 6 vectors in R4\R^4R4, it must span R4\R^4R4.

False. At least four linearly independent vectors are needed to span R4\R^4R4. If at least four of the six are the same or are multiples of each other, then they cannot R4\R^4R4.

(c) Suppose that there are 4 vectors in R6\R^6R6, it must be linearly independent.

False. If they are not distinct vectors or are multiples of each other, then they would be linearly dependent.

(d) Suppose that there are 4 vectors in R6\R^6R6, it cannot span R6\R^6R6.

True. At least six linearly independent vectors are needed to span R6\R^6R6.
